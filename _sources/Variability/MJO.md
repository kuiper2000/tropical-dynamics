# Week 8: Madden-Julian oscillation
## Weak Temperature and Steady State Response 


MJO is a unique feature existing in the tropical Indian Ocean and the Western Pacific, which characterized by planetary scale circulation (zonal wave number around $1-4$ and ) intraseasonal timescales (20-90 days of period). That's why it's also called _tropical intraseasonal variability_.
